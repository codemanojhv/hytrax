# Code of Conduct

Hytrax is a welcoming project. Participants must be respectful and must not engage in
harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, or unwanted sexual attention.

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when
conduct harms the project or its contributors.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer listed in `package.json` or through
GitHub's private contact options. Do not create a public issue for sensitive reports.
